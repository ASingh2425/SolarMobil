Our Subsystems

Discover the specialized teams that form the backbone of SolarMobil, from core engineering to strategic planning and outreach.

Engineering

Mechanical

Vehicle Dynamics

Handles braking, steering, and suspension systems. We ensure the car is stable, safe, and handles precisely on the track.

Structures

Designs and manufactures the chassis and bodywork using advanced composites and lightweight materials to ensure structural integrity.

Engineering

Electrical & Electronics

Battery and BMS

Designs and integrates the high-voltage battery pack, ensuring safety, reliability, and optimal power delivery.

Solar Array

Focuses on MPPTs and solar panel integration, maximizing energy capture from the sun to power the vehicle.

Motor & Control

Manages the electric motor and motor controller, translating battery power into efficient and responsive wheel propulsion.

Data Acquisition

Develops sensor networks (CAN) and telemetry systems to monitor vehicle health and performance in real-time.

Power Distribution

Integrates all low-voltage and high-voltage systems, designing custom PCBs and wiring harnesses for reliable power flow.

Analytics

Research & Strategy

Research

A research-focused team aiming to publish papers and file patents for our innovations, pushing the boundaries of solar-electric mobility.

Strategy

Builds models to calculate optimal race speed based on solar input, battery charge, terrain, and weather to guide race efficiency.

Operations

Management

Logistics

Oversees logistics, finance, and external communication, ensuring smooth coordination and keeping the project on track.

Marketing

Manages the team's brand identity, social media presence, and public outreach to connect with our community and partners.

Sponsorship

Builds and maintains relationships with our valued partners, securing the resources needed to innovate and compete.
