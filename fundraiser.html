Our Mission: The Bridgestone World Solar Challenge

SolarMobil Manipal, a team of passionate engineering students, is on a mission to design, build, and race a solar-powered electric vehicle at the prestigious Bridgestone World Solar Challenge (BWSC) in 2027. We are developing 'HELIARK', our 7th generation car, to compete in the world's toughest solar endurance race.

The BWSC is a 3,000-kilometer journey across the Australian outback, pushing the limits of technology, innovation, and sustainable engineering. Our goal is not just to compete, but to showcase India's potential in green technology on a global stage.

Why We Need Your Support

Building a world-class solar car from scratch is a massive undertaking, both technically and financially. While our team dedicates countless hours to design, research, and fabrication, the specialized components required are expensive. Your contribution will directly fund the critical systems that make a solar car competitive.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Every rupee you donate will be meticulously allocated to the car's core components. Here’s a breakdown of our primary costs:

  • High-Efficiency Solar Array: The heart of our vehicle, costing approximately ₹5-6 Lakhs.
  • Custom Battery Pack: For energy storage and endurance, estimated at ₹4-5 Lakhs.
  • Carbon Fibre & Composites: To build an ultra-lightweight and aerodynamic chassis, costing ₹3-4 Lakhs.
  • In-Wheel Motor & Controller: The powerhouse for efficient propulsion, estimated at ₹3-4 Lakhs.
  • Logistics & Shipping: Safely transporting our car and team to Australia is a major expense, at ₹7-8 Lakhs.
