HELIARK

Challenger Class Vehicle

Specifications

Motor
3kW, 96V BLDC
Battery
96V, 25Ah Lithium-Ion
Solar Cells
890W, Monocrystalline (Ga-doped)
Chassis
Tubular Space Frame (Aluminium)
Body
Carbon Fibre Hybrid Composite
Suspension
Double Wishbone (Front & Rear)
Brakes
Regenerative & Hydraulic Disc
Weight
170 kg
Top Speed
90 km/h

Description

HELIARK is our latest and most advanced challenger class vehicle, unveiled in 2024. It represents the culmination of over a decade of learning, innovation, and 'imagineering' from all our previous projects.

Building on the success of ZENITH, HELIARK features a refined 'bullet' style aerodynamic profile and an ultra-lightweight aluminium space frame chassis. The body is a state-of-the-art carbon fibre composite, further reducing weight while maximizing structural integrity.

It carries forward the successful 890W solar array and 3kW motor configuration, but with significant improvements in power electronics, battery management, and predictive telemetry. HELIARK is the embodiment of our team's commitment to pushing the boundaries of solar technology and competitive racing.
