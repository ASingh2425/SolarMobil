About SolarMobil

We are a team of passionate students and engineers dedicated to advancing solar-powered vehicle technology through innovation, education, and sustainable engineering.

Mission Image Placeholder

Our Mission

To design and build the future of sustainable transportation.

We aim to create cutting-edge solar vehicles that demonstrate the viability of clean energy, while educating future engineers and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Vision Image Placeholder

Our Vision

To be a leading force in solar vehicle innovation.

We envision a world where solar power is a practical solution for daily mobility, inspiring the next generation to build a cleaner, more efficient future.

Our Journey

Milestones on the Road

2018

Foundation

SolarMobil is established at Manipal Institute of Technology with a vision to develop sustainable transportation solutions.

2019

First Prototype

Development of our first solar vehicle prototype, focusing on basic electrical systems and lightweight materials.

2021

Advanced Systems

Implementation of advanced battery management systems, improved MPPT controllers, and enhanced safety features.

2023

Competition Success

Participation in national-level solar vehicle competitions with recognition for innovative design.
