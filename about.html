About SolarMobil

MAHE's official student Solar Vehicle Team, established in 2011. We design, build, and race solar-electric vehicles for global competitions, pushing the boundaries of sustainable transportation.

Our Mission

To design and build cutting-edge solar-powered vehicles.

Our goal is to demonstrate the viability of sustainable transportation while educating future engineers and pushing the boundaries of renewable energy technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in sustainable vehicle innovation.

We aim to inspire the next generation of engineers to create a cleaner, more efficient transportation future powered by renewable energy, transforming concepts into practical solutions.

Our Journey

Timeline

2011

Foundation

SolarMobil established at MIT, Manipal. Initial team formation and planning.

2013

FREYR-1

First prototype, a single-seater adventure class vehicle, completed.

2015

SERVe

Launched India's first passenger solar electric vehicle prototype. Won QuEST Ingenium.

2016

SM S-1

Developed India's first four-seater family solar passenger car.

2018

SM S-2

Launched a two-seater cruiser class car aimed at the World Solar Challenge.

2023

ZENITH

Competed in ESVC 2023 with a new bullet-design car featuring composite materials.

2024

HELIARK

Our latest generation vehicle, representing the pinnacle of our engineering efforts to date.

2026

Future Goals

Currently developing our 7th generation vehicle for the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge 2027.
